POST VASECTOMY SPERM COUNTER
  • MIX THE SAMPLE THOROUGHLY AND PREPARE A SLIDE
  • INSERT SLIDE INTO THE VISUALIZATION SLOT, FOCUS ON FULL ZOOM OUT
  • SCAN 50 FIELDS OF VIEW PER SLIDE; ENTER THE # SLIDES ASSESSED
  • CLICK THE MOTILE / IMMOTILE BUTTONS TO COUNT
  • WHEN FINISHED CLICK: RESULTS
